Who am I?

I am a software engineer from Adelaide, Australia. I love building things, and solving problems, through code.
I like to understand programming and computing from the lowest level, even when coding at higher levels.

Why is this website so simple?

I like flashy and beautiful websites, but it didn't feel right to me to not be in control of the code of my own website.
I wanted to write it from scratch, and understand everything I did, something that frameworks would take away from me.

I also wanted the focus to be mainly on my code and output.
